Parametric is an interactive digital artwork that investigates our individual and collective emotional state in a post isolated world to create unique pieces of art. You are invited to input your feelings on a sliding scale across a series of questions about how you feel about your life and relationships. Each input changes the artwork and renders a morphing and shifting piece of art on the screen before your very eyes. Enter
